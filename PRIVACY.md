# Privacy Policy — Onyx Digital Platform

**Effective date:** April 2026

---

## 1. Who we are

Onyx Digital Platform is a private social media growth agency operating in Kenya. This tool is used internally to manage client accounts and generate AI-powered content.

## 2. What information we collect from clients

When you sign on as a client of Onyx Digital Platform, we collect:

- Your name and brand name
- Your contact information (phone number or email)
- Your social media handles and platform access (via Business Manager — no passwords)
- Your follower counts and engagement metrics for reporting purposes
- Your payment details for invoicing (M-Pesa or bank transfer — not stored in this tool)

## 3. How we use your information

We use your information solely to:

- Manage and grow your social media accounts
- Generate content tailored to your brand
- Produce monthly performance reports
- Contact you regarding your subscription and renewal

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your information with any third party.

## 4. How your data is stored

Client data entered into the Onyx tool is stored locally in the browser (localStorage) of the Onyx operator's device. It is not uploaded to any cloud database. Your data stays on our internal device only.

## 5. Your account access

We access your social media accounts only through official business access tools:

- **Instagram/Facebook** — via Meta Business Suite (Editor role, no password sharing)
- **TikTok** — via TikTok Business Center (Collaborator access)
- **YouTube** — via YouTube Studio (Manager access)

You can revoke our access at any time through these platforms.

## 6. Your rights

You have the right to:

- Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your data at any time
- Revoke social media access at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via WhatsApp or email as per your client agreement.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the business grows. Any significant changes will be communicated to active clients directly.
