Standard operating procedure
1
Sign the client
Agree on package. Get WhatsApp or email confirmation. Collect: brand name, niche, tone, target audience, all platform handles, and whether they have an ad budget. Create a shared WhatsApp group for updates.
2
Get access to their accounts (safely)
Instagram/Facebook: ask them to add you as an Editor in Meta Business Suite — they never share their password. TikTok: use TikTok Business Center and request collaborator access. YouTube: they add you as Manager in YouTube Studio.
Meta Business SuiteTikTok Business CenterYouTube Studio
3
Generate content — use the Generate tab
Fill in client niche, tone, audience, platforms, and goal. Click Generate. Gemini AI writes 30 posts with viral hooks and hashtags. Download the CSV and import into Buffer for auto-scheduling.
Onyx Generate tabBuffer CSV import
4
Schedule posts in Buffer
Go to buffer.com → Import CSV → Connect client's platforms → Set posting schedule: Mon / Wed / Fri / Sat at 7AM, 12PM, 7PM EAT (peak engagement). Buffer posts automatically. You check once a week — takes 15 minutes.
buffer.com
5
Set up paid ads (if client has budget)
Use the Ad Briefs tab to generate the campaign strategy. Go to Meta Ads Manager or TikTok Ads Manager. Build the campaign using the brief. For TikTok: use Spark Ads to boost existing posts. For Instagram: boost the best-performing post each week. You charge 15% of ad spend as your management fee.
Meta Ads ManagerTikTok Spark AdsGoogle Ads
6
Monitor weekly (15 minutes per client)
Check Buffer to confirm posts went out. Open Meta Business Suite insights → note reach and engagement numbers. Pause any ad spending with zero results. Screenshot the best performing post of the week.
7
Send monthly report
Download follower count, reach, and engagement from each platform. Use the Report Generator below. Send via WhatsApp or email. Include best 3 posts, total follower growth, and a plan for next month.
Report Generator (below)
8
Collect renewal payment
3 days before month-end, send via WhatsApp: "Hi [name], your Onyx report is ready. You gained [X] followers this month. Renewing for next month?" Collect payment via M-Pesa or bank transfer before starting the next month's content.
